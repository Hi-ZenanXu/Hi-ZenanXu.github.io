    Biography

    Zenen Xu, advised by Prof. Qinliang Su and Xiaojun Quan, is a second year PHD candidate in computer science at Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU). He received the bachelor's degree of computer science in 2017 from SYSU. Then he took the national graduate entrance examination, ranked first with full marks in advanced mathematics, and studied at SYSU again. Under the guidance of Prof. Yanghui Rao for two years, he obtained a master's degree in 2019. In the first year of his PhD candidate, he took his intership in Microsoft Research Asia and the corresponding supervisors are Dr. Duyu Tang, Dr. Linjun Shou, and Dr. Ming Gong.

    Research interests:

    Zenan Xu mainly interests in Natural language processing (NLP) (particularly in Pre-trained Model) and Graphs mining (especially in Dynamic/Static Attributed Graph Embedding). He also focuses on applying the graph technology to help advance NLP.
